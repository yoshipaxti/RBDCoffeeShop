Welcome to RBD Coffee Shop!

We are a locally-owned coffee shop that takes pride in serving the finest, freshly brewed coffee in town. Our baristas are passionate about delivering the perfect cup of joe every time, and we use only premium quality beans sourced from sustainable farms across the world.

At our cozy and welcoming cafe, you'll find a menu filled with deliciously crafted espresso drinks, specialty lattes, and teas.

Whether you're looking for a quick morning pick-me-up or a relaxing afternoon treat, our friendly staff is always here to make your experience an enjoyable one. Come and join us at our coffee shop and discover why we're the go-to spot for coffee lovers in town!

Join Our Team

Explore exciting job opportunities at our coffee shop.

Barista

Responsibilities:


Prepare and serve high-quality coffee beverages to customers
Operate espresso machines and other equipment
Provide exceptional customer service in a fast-paced environment
Handle cash transactions and maintain accurate records
Ensure cleanliness and sanitation of the work area
Prior barista experience preferred but not required

Shift Supervisor

Responsibilities:


Supervise and train baristas on coffee preparation, customer service, and store procedures
Assist with inventory management and ordering supplies
Maintain a clean and organized store environment
Handle cash transactions and reconcile daily sales reports
Resolve customer complaints and ensure high levels of customer satisfaction
Prior supervisory experience in a coffee shop or related field preferred

if you're interested in applying for one of these positions, please send your resume and cover letter to RBDcoffeeshop@ccmail.uk. We look forward to hearing from you!
